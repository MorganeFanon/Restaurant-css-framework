The restaurant was opened in 1978 by Shirley Macddoyle. Shirley grew up in the vast prairies of Ohio and moved to Belgium in the early 1970s. A fan of the 60's, she opened this typical American diner with her husband Steve. Within five years this diner became The Place to be in Brussels. Despite this dazzling success, Steve never really managed to acclimatize and was homesick. He left Shirley and moved back to the United States, where he lives very happily today. He remarried and has since had five children. Steve is unemployed and is being supported by his new wife. But we're getting off track... despite all these great misfortunes, Shirley didn't let herself be and worked day and night to make this place as magical as it is today. today. Your visit to this enchanting place will not leave you indifferent... Shirley guarantees it!
